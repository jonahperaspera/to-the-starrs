FAQ

Are children welcome?

We love your little ones, and their presence makes the church more alive! Children are welcome at the ceremony.

What should I wear?

Garden formal — think summer dresses, linen suits, or cocktail attire. Since part of the evening is on grass, we recommend block heels or flats. May in Hillsdale can be occasionally rainy in the evenings; a light layer is a good idea.
